CMIS- Revolutionalizing Asset Maintenance

Computerized asset maintenance and parts inventory system has gained traction in the current industrial setups. Our system is a web based application that is geared towards streamlining the maintenance activities in a bit to realize the benefits of a reliability centered maintenance. The software was build with keen attention to the requirements of TPM. It has the following features:

1. Parts Inventory

2. Work order management

3. Preventive maintenance

4. Breakdown maintenance

5. Abnormalities Management

6. Maintenance Costing

7. Reporting

8. Statistical/Graphical Analysis

9. Asset Tracking

10. Team Management

11. Automated Parts Requisition

11. Automated Notification System
